About Me

Welcome to Walter in the Woods, I am Walter a recent college graduate who is hiking the Appalachian Trail headed South Bound (SOBO), and I am using this website as a way to document my journey! I summited Mt. Katahdin on July 27th, 2024, and am pushing forward towards Waynesboro VA, where I will help volunteer with the damage from hurricane Helene.
